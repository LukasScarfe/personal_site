There are a million varieties of [Coleus plant](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coleus). I have no idea which one this is, I might have taken a small piece from another plant when we were at [Supercrawl](https://supercrawl.ca/) in Hamilton in the Fall of '24. These are most often annuals that people will just buy every year and keep outside, but I have found that this one really thrives being in bright light indoors. It has stuck around for a couple years now, and it doesn't seem to be slowing down. This is a great plant for propagation, every time I cut it back I end up with more than I need, and like a Hydra, for every branch that is cut, 2 grow back. It has gone from a little weed to a full on bush with a woody stem.
